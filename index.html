CopyRight Law

Purpose

The basis for copyright protection stems directly from the U.S. Constitution. The Founding Fathers understood the importance of being able to secure the rights of authors, scientists, and artists for a limited time.

Requirements

The work must be original, creative, and meet the fixation requirement a work must be fixed in a tangible medium of expression.

Protected Work

  • Musical Works
  • Literary Works
  • Motion Picture Works
  • Sound Recording
  • Dramatic Works
  • Pictorial, Graphic, and Sculptural Works

Unprotected Work

  • Titles and Slogans
  • Ideas,Facts, and Concepts
  • List of ingredients
  • Familiar symbols
  • Simple Geometric Shapes
  • Processes and Procedures

Copyright Notice

  • Copyright Symbol
  • Year of Publication
  • Copyright Owner's Name
